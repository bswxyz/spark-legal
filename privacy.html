Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Cozely ("we," "us") is a game for couples to play together. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information we collect

AccountYour email address, used to sign in (handled by our auth provider, Clerk). A display name you choose.
Couple contentPhotos and optional voice notes you save as keepsakes, captions/notes you write, and the answers and choices you make during games.
GameplayWhich games you play, scores, streaks, and the themes you type when you ask the in‑app host to customize a game.
PurchasesIf you subscribe to Cozely Premium, Apple processes the payment. We receive your subscription status, not your card details.

We do not use third‑party analytics, advertising, or crash‑tracking SDKs, and we do not collect your location, contacts, or device advertising identifier.

Device permissions

Cozely asks for these only when you use the feature, and you can change them anytime in iOS Settings:

CameraTo snap a reaction selfie or keepsake photo during a game.
Photo LibraryTo add a photo you already have to a keepsake.
MicrophoneTo record an optional short voice note on a keepsake.
NotificationsFor your optional weekly date‑night reminder.

How we use it

  • To run the game between you and your partner and keep both phones in sync.
  • To store your keepsakes and let you revisit, caption, share, or delete them.
  • To personalize the in‑app host ("Juno") — for Premium couples, your names and recent answers may be sent to our AI provider, Anthropic (Claude), to write warmer, on‑theme prompts. See "AI features & our AI providers" below.
  • To provide and improve the service and respond to support requests.

Who we share it with (service providers)

We share data only with the processors that run the app, under their own terms:

ClerkSign‑in and account management (your email and user ID).
ConvexOur backend and file storage (your names, photos, voice notes, and gameplay data).
Anthropic (Claude)The AI host. Receives game prompts, your names, and the answers needed to judge a round, write a personalized line, or generate on‑theme prompts. If you submit a photo as dare "proof," that image is sent to be reviewed. Not used to train models on your data.
ElevenLabsOptional voice for the host. Receives the host's spoken line to convert to audio.
AppleProcesses subscription payments and provides the App Store.

AI features & our AI providers

Some Cozely features are powered by third‑party artificial‑intelligence services. When you use them, the specific data below is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to the provider named, used only to generate that response in the moment, and — as confirmed in each provider's terms — not used to train their models on your data:

Anthropic (Claude)Powers the in‑app host "Juno." Receives the game prompt, you and your partner's display names, your chosen vibe, and the answers or choices needed to judge a round, write a personalized line, or generate an on‑theme round set. If you submit a photo as dare "proof," that image is sent to Claude solely to review it for the game. Sent only when you trigger an AI‑assisted moment.
ElevenLabsOptional spoken voice for the host. Receives only the host's already‑written line of text to convert into audio. It does not receive your photos.

We share this data with Anthropic and ElevenLabs strictly as our processors, under written agreements that require them to protect it with confidentiality, security, and data‑handling safeguards at least equal to the protections described in this policy, to use it only to provide the feature back to us, and not to sell it, use it for their own advertising, or train their models on it. Both providers are based in the United States; where data is transferred internationally we rely on their safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses). We ask for your explicit permission before any personal data is sent to these providers (during onboarding, and again anytime in Settings → Your AI Host). If you decline or turn sharing off, games still work using built‑in library content and on‑device voice — nothing is sent to Anthropic or ElevenLabs.

Sharing a recap

If you choose to share a game recap, we create a public link that shows the recap card and your two profile photos. The link contains no account access. You control whether to share it.

Your choices & deletion

  • Delete a memory: remove any photo or whole keepsake in the app at any time.
  • Delete your account: Settings → Delete account permanently removes your couple's profile, photos, voice notes, and game history.
  • Subscriptions: manage or cancel anytime in your Apple Account settings.

Data retention & security

We keep your data only as long as we need it:

Account & couple dataKept while your account is active. When you delete your account, it is removed from our active systems right away and from routine backups within about 30 days.
Keepsakes (photos, voice notes, captions)Kept until you delete the item or your account.
AI requestsYour names and answers are sent to our AI providers (Anthropic and, for the optional spoken voice, ElevenLabs) only to produce a response in the moment; they are not used to train their models.
Waitlist emailIf you joined the pre‑launch waitlist, we keep your email until launch or until you ask us to remove it.

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored with our backend provider. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Your privacy rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Cozely is operated by an individual based in the United States, who is the data controller for the personal data described here; contact support@tdle.win. Our legal bases for processing are:

  • Performance of a contract — to run the game between you and your partner and provide the service you signed up for.
  • Consent — for optional features such as AI personalization, notifications, and unlocking adult ("spicy") content. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and improve how it works.

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to access, correct, delete, port, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent. You may exercise these rights — most are also available directly in the app — by emailing support@tdle.win. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data‑protection authority. Our providers are based in the United States; where personal data is transferred internationally, we rely on those providers' safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).
