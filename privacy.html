Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

SPARK ("we," "us") is a game for couples to play together. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Information we collect

AccountYour email address, used to sign in (handled by our auth provider, Clerk). A display name you choose.
Couple contentPhotos and optional voice notes you save as keepsakes, captions/notes you write, and the answers and choices you make during games.
GameplayWhich games you play, scores, streaks, and the themes you type when you ask the in‑app host to customize a game.
PurchasesIf you subscribe to SPARK Premium, Apple processes the payment. We receive your subscription status, not your card details.

We do not use third‑party analytics, advertising, or crash‑tracking SDKs, and we do not collect your location, contacts, or device advertising identifier.

Device permissions

SPARK asks for these only when you use the feature, and you can change them anytime in iOS Settings:

CameraTo snap a reaction selfie or keepsake photo during a game.
Photo LibraryTo add a photo you already have to a keepsake.
MicrophoneTo record an optional short voice note on a keepsake.
NotificationsFor your optional weekly date‑night reminder.

How we use it

  • To run the game between you and your partner and keep both phones in sync.
  • To store your keepsakes and let you revisit, caption, share, or delete them.
  • To personalize the in‑app host ("Juno") — for Premium couples, your names and recent answers may be sent to our AI provider to write warmer, on‑theme prompts.
  • To provide and improve the service and respond to support requests.

Who we share it with (service providers)

We share data only with the processors that run the app, under their own terms:

ClerkSign‑in and account management (your email and user ID).
ConvexOur backend and file storage (your names, photos, voice notes, and gameplay data).
Anthropic (Claude)The AI host. Receives game prompts, your names, and the answers needed to judge a round, write a personalized line, or generate on‑theme prompts. If you submit a photo as dare "proof," that image is sent to be reviewed. Not used to train models on your data.
ElevenLabsOptional voice for the host. Receives the host's spoken line to convert to audio.
AppleProcesses subscription payments and provides the App Store.

Sharing a recap

If you choose to share a game recap, we create a public link that shows the recap card and your two profile photos. The link contains no account access. You control whether to share it.

Your choices & deletion

  • Delete a memory: remove any photo or whole keepsake in the app at any time.
  • Delete your account: Settings → Delete account permanently removes your couple's profile, photos, voice notes, and game history.
  • Subscriptions: manage or cancel anytime in your Apple Account settings.

Data retention & security

We keep your data only as long as we need it:

Account & couple dataKept while your account is active. When you delete your account, it is removed from our active systems right away and from routine backups within about 30 days.
Keepsakes (photos, voice notes, captions)Kept until you delete the item or your account.
AI requestsYour names and answers are sent to our AI provider only to produce a response in the moment; they are not used to train its models.
Waitlist emailIf you joined the pre‑launch waitlist, we keep your email until launch or until you ask us to remove it.

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored with our backend provider. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Your privacy rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

SPARK is operated by an individual based in the United States, who is the data controller for the personal data described here; contact support@sparkcouples.com. Our legal bases for processing are:

  • Performance of a contract — to run the game between you and your partner and provide the service you signed up for.
  • Consent — for optional features such as AI personalization, notifications, and unlocking adult ("spicy") content. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and improve how it works.

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to access, correct, delete, port, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent. You may exercise these rights — most are also available directly in the app — by emailing support@sparkcouples.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data‑protection authority. Our providers are based in the United States; where personal data is transferred internationally, we rely on those providers' safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).

Your California privacy rights (CCPA / CPRA)

To make a request — or to use an authorized agent — email support@sparkcouples.com. We will verify your request using your account email before acting on it.
