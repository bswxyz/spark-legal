Support

Cozely is a date‑night game you play together on two phones.

How do we start?

One partner taps Start a new couple and shares the code. The other taps Join with a code and enters it. Pick a game and play. Prefer one phone? Several games have a "Play on one phone" mode.

What's free, and what's Premium?

The core games and your keepsake scrapbook are free — including a few of Juno's live AI reactions every day. Cozely Premium adds unlimited AI reactions, every game and deck, custom AI themes, spicy mode, and unlimited memories. It's Weekly or Yearly, each with a 3‑day free trial.

How do I manage my subscription?

Cozely Premium renews through your Apple Account. Manage or cancel anytime in iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my data?

Remove any photo or memory in the app, or fully erase your account in Settings → Delete account. Deletion is permanent for both partners.
