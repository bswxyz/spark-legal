Date‑night ideas for nights you'd rather stay in

No reservation, no babysitter, no pants required. Twelve ways to make an ordinary night at home feel like a real date.

  1. Play a question game. Trade your phones for one round of "ask me anything" and see how far you get. (This is literally what we built Cozely for.)
  2. Cook one dish together, badly. Pick something neither of you has made. The mess is the memory.
  3. Recreate your first date — the meal, the playlist, the outfit if you're feeling it.
  4. Build a two‑person blanket fort and watch the comfort movie you've both seen ten times.
  5. Do a "highs and lows" of the year over a drink — then make three predictions for next year.
  6. Give each other a 10‑minute spa. Hand massage, good lotion, phones in the other room.
  7. Plan a dream trip you can't afford yet. Pick the city, the hotel, the first meal. Dreaming together counts.
  8. Truth or dare, grown‑up edition. Keep it playful, let it warm up.
  9. Make a couple's playlist — one song each, taking turns, no skipping.
  10. Write each other a one‑line love note and hide it somewhere they'll find it tomorrow.
  11. Do a blind taste test — snacks, sauces, wines — and argue about it.
  12. Take one good photo together and actually print it. A night you can hold beats a night you forget.
